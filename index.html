About:

  • This is an informal page about CASS MSA TC. See its official web page, and committee roster.
  • MSA TC is one of the 14 TCs in IEEE CASS. This TC is dedicated to promote the information exchange of pertaining to the research, development, and practice of multimeida technologies, systems and applications.
  • TC commitments
    • Dedicated MSA track for publications in ISCAS
    • Assist and improve other CAS-sponsored conferences such as ICME (1/4)
    • Steering committee of IEEE TMM (1/4)
    • Paper review in TMM, T-CSVT, T-VLSI, T-CAS I & II
    • Nominate individuals for various awards and posts
    • Nominate best paper awards
    • Prepare TC Annual Report
    • Prepare periodic CASS review
    • Maintaining TC charter and website

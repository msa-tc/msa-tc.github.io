Fast track towards IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (IEEE TMM):

  • Qualified papers submitted to Track 12 (MSA) will be recommended to be extended to full papers towards IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (TMM). A three-month fast reviewing track will be used.
  • An Email notification will be sent to authors of accepted papers in Track 12 to confirm their intention to participate in this competition.
  • A subcommittee in MSA TC will select the qualified papers to be accepted in the fast track.
  • A Guest Associate Edtior team of IEEE TMM will be in charge of the reviewing process of the selected paper.

  • Best paper awards will be selected in papers submitted to Track 12 (MSA).
